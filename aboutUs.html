About Us

The vendor’s credentials: architect with a passion for early Motto Guzzi’s motorcycles and motorcycling, in particular long runs (recently Scotland - Finland – Russia) and crossing borders. The clarity of mind it gives. The intensity of the trip. The field of vision funneling into the immediate. You are not on the road, you are in it, a part of it. I am very bike specific and also ride Guzzi California 850 T3 (78) and XT 500 (78)

We specialize in sourcing, restoring, and selling rare and iconic motorcycles from the golden age of biking. Every bike we work on is treated with care, respect, and attention to detail — blending craftsmanship with heritage.

Whether you're a collector, a rider, or just an admirer of classic design, we invite you to explore our collection and join us in keeping the spirit of vintage motorcycles alive.
